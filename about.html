About

Tracy Jane Toledo, MScA, RD

How I can help

I started my private practice in downtown Toronto in the fall of 2018, shortly after completing my dietetic internship with McGill University. After returning home from Montreal, I began my journey as a practicing Registered Dietitian by combining my entrepreneurial spirit with my license to counsel for nutritional management of chronic health conditions.

With my office space in the heart of city, I discovered a need from people seeking help predominantly for Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS), Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) and weight loss, and so I gravitated towards specializing in nutrition therapies for digestive health, womenâ€™s health conditions and sustainable weight management.

To ensure an optimal experience for my clients and to truly achieve better health through food and habits, I continue to pursue knowledge and understanding of emerging nutrition therapies and approaches to behaviour change. As a result, I incorporate mindful and intuitive eating principles in my sessions to help my clients find balance and ultimately improve their health and relationship with food, mind and body.
