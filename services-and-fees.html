Session Expectations

Learn how to create satisfying and sustainable ways of eating that support your health condition.


All sessions are offered virtually, either by phone or online video chat. Supporting resources and nutrition plans are sent via a secure online platform.


1 hour Initial Assessmsent: Comprehensive review of your medical history, lifestyle and usual food intake. Evalutation of health and habits. Goal setting and creating an action plan, completed in session. Personalized diet and lifestyle recommendations, balanced meal and snack guidelines are provided.


30 minute Follow-up: Monitoring progress, facilitating diet modifications and continued food recommendations to support symtpom or lifetsyle management. Buildling on your action plan to help meet your overall goal. Follow-ups are recommended every 1-4 weeks depending on how frequent you prefer support and accountability.

Insurance Coverage

Many extended benefits through private health insurance plans cover nutrition counselling by a Registered Dietitian. Contact your HR department to learn what your plan includes.

  • Direct billing is available for the following insurance plans: Canada Life, Great-West Life, Chambers of Commerce, CINUP, ClaimSecure, First Canadian, Industrial Alliance, Johnston group, Maximum Benefit
  • For all other insurance plans, payment is made out-of-pocket and individualized receipts for health insurance purposes will be provided after each appointment with the date and time of service provided
  • For persons without coverage, fees may be charged on a sliding scale
