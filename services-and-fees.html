Insurance Coverage

Many extended benefits through private health insurance plans cover nutrition counselling by a Registered Dietitian. Contact your HR department to learn what your plan includes.

  • Direct billing is available for the following insurance plans: Canada Life, Great-West Life, Chambers of Commerce, CINUP, ClaimSecure, First Canadian, Industrial Alliance, Johnston group, Maximum Benefit
  • For all other insurance plans, payment is made out-of-pocket and individualized receipts for health insurance purposes will be provided after each appointment with the date and time of service provided
  • For persons without coverage, fees may be charged on a sliding scale
